Our Name

Long ago, a young Catholic Priest was commissioned by the Pope to evangelize Ireland. Patrick began looking for ways to communicate to a group of people who knew nothing about the Christian faith. In a moment of inspiration, he used a three leaf clover to help explain the concept of the Holy Trinity.

Like St. Patrick, you have a message to communicate. Let us help you take your message to the masses. Let us help you find your three leaf clover.
